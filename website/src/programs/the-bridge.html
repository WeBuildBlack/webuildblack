THE BRIDGE

Connecting where you are to where you want to be.

What is The Bridge?

The Bridge is an 8-week virtual accountability program where small pods of 4-5 WBB members support each other through the tech interview process. You'll be grouped by target role, meet weekly on your own terms, and receive personalized coaching insights via Slack every week.

The job search is isolating. The Bridge fixes that. You get a crew of people going through the same thing — practicing together, applying together, and pushing each other to the finish line.

Apply Now Join our Slack

How It Works

  1. Apply & Get Matched. Fill out the application. We group you into a pod of 4-5 people targeting similar roles — frontend, backend, data, design, product, or mobile.
  2. Meet Weekly. Your Way. Your pod meets once a week over Google Meet. You choose when, how long, and what you work on — mock interviews, leetcode sessions, application sprints, resume reviews, or interview debriefs.
  3. Track Your Progress. Log your weekly activity in Notion — problems practiced, applications sent, interviews scheduled, wins, and blockers. Takes 5 minutes.
  4. Get Personalized Coaching. Every Monday, you'll get a Slack DM with insights based on your data — what's working, where to focus, and a nudge when you need it. No human overhead. Just smart, automated support.

What Do Pods Do Together?

Your pod picks from a menu of session formats each week. Here are some options:

  • Mock Interview Pairs — Take turns interviewing each other (behavioral or technical)
  • Leetcode Together — Solve 2-3 problems as a group, discuss approaches
  • System Design Jam — One person presents a design, the group critiques
  • Application Sprint — Everyone applies to 3-5 jobs on the call together
  • Resume & Portfolio Roast — Constructive feedback on each other's materials
  • Interview Debrief — Share experiences from real interviews that week
  • Salary & Negotiation Prep — Research comp ranges, practice negotiation scripts

The 8-Week Journey

Week 0 — Kickoff

All pods gather for a virtual kickoff. Meet your pod, set your goals, schedule your first meeting. This is the only session WBB hosts live.

Weeks 1-2 — Foundation

Assess where you are. Build your study plan. Review resumes. Create your target company list. Establish your pod's rhythm.

Weeks 3-6 — The Grind

This is where the work happens. Practice DSA, system design, and behavioral questions. Send applications. Do mock interviews. Your weekly coaching DMs keep you focused and balanced.

Weeks 7-8 — Final Sprint

Push hard on applications and interviews. Do a retrospective with your pod. Celebrate your growth. Exchange contact info to stay connected.

What You Get

  • A pod of 4-5 people in your target role who have your back
  • Weekly personalized coaching insights via Slack DM
  • A structured 8-week framework to keep you accountable
  • A menu of proven pod session formats to choose from
  • Access to the full WBB Slack community and #jobs channel
  • A digital certificate of completion when you graduate
