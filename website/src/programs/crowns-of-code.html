Crowns of Code

Youth Coding Program

Inspiring the Next Generation

Crowns of Code introduces young people in the Black community to computer science fundamentals through hands-on, project-based learning. Our goal is to build confidence, spark curiosity, and create a culturally affirming space where young coders can thrive.

Students learn programming concepts through interactive projects, building real applications they can share with friends and family. Our curriculum is designed to meet students where they are — no prior experience needed.
