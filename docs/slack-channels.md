# Slack Channel Conventions

## Naming
- `#general` — Community-wide discussion
- `#announcements` — Official WBB announcements (admin-post only)
- `#introductions` — New members introduce themselves
- `#cohort-{year}-{quarter}` — Cohort-specific channels (e.g., #cohort-2026-q1)
- `#track-{name}` — Track-specific discussion (e.g., #track-android-dev)
- `#mentors` — Mentor coordination
- `#events` — Event announcements and discussion
- `#jobs` — Job postings and career opportunities
- `#random` — Off-topic community chat

## The Bridge Program
- `#bridge-{year}-{season}` — All-cohort announcements (e.g., #bridge-2026-spring)
- `#bridge-{year}-{season}-{role}` — Pod channels (e.g., #bridge-2026-spring-frontend, #bridge-2026-spring-data)
