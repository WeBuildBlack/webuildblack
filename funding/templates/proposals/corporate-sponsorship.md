## About We Build Black

### Who We Are

We Build Black (WBB) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit founded in 2017 in Brooklyn, NY. What started in 2016 as the Black Software Engineers of NYC Meetup has grown into a multi-program organization dedicated to empowering the Black community through technical education and professional development.
