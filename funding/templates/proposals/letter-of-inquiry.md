## Organization Overview

We Build Black (WBB) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization founded in 2017 and based in Brooklyn, NY at 147 Front Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201. Originally launched in 2016 as the Black Software Engineers of NYC Meetup, WBB has grown into a community-driven organization that empowers the Black community to achieve socio-economic change through technical education and professional development.

Our programs serve {{ANNUAL_PARTICIPANTS}} participants annually and include workforce training, youth coding education, interview preparation, a technology conference, and a dedicated women's chapter. Since inception, WBB has been supported by partners including Google, JP Morgan Chase, The New York Times, Shopify, and Microsoft.
