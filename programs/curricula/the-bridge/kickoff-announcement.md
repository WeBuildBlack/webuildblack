:bridge_at_night: **Introducing The Bridge — A New WBB Program**

We're launching something new and we need you.

**The Bridge** is an 8-week virtual accountability program for WBB members who are actively job searching or preparing for tech interviews. Here's the idea: we put you in a small pod of 4-5 people targeting similar roles, you meet weekly on your own schedule, and we send you personalized coaching insights every Monday based on your progress.

**Why?**
The job search is isolating. You're grinding leetcode alone, sending applications into the void, and wondering if you're doing enough. The Bridge fixes that. You get a crew of people going through the same thing — practicing together, applying together, and pushing each other to the finish line.

**How it works:**
:one: You apply and we match you into a pod by target role (frontend, backend, data, design, mobile, or product)
:two: Your pod meets once a week on Google Meet — you decide when, how long, and what you work on (mock interviews, leetcode, application sprints, resume reviews, etc.)
:three: You spend 5 min/week logging your progress in a Notion tracker
:four: Every Monday you get a personalized Slack DM with coaching insights — what's working, where to focus, and a nudge when you need it

**What you get:**
- A pod of 4-5 people who have your back through the search
- Weekly personalized coaching via Slack DM
- A structured 8-week framework to stay accountable
- Access to the full WBB community and #jobs channel
- A digital certificate when you graduate

**The details:**
- Runs 3x/year: January, May, September (aligned with hiring seasons)
- 8 weeks, fully virtual, completely free
- All experience levels welcome
- Space is limited to ~30 members per cohort

**Graduation criteria:**
- Log progress at least 6 of 8 weeks
- Attend at least 6 pod meetings
- Complete at least 4 mock interviews
- Submit at least 10 job applications
