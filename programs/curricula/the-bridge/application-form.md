> Create this form at forms.google.com. Once created, replace the placeholder URL

> and in the Slack kickoff announcement.

---

## Form Title
**Apply for The Bridge — WBB Interview Accountability Program**

## Form Description
The Bridge is an 8-week virtual accountability program where small pods of 4-5 WBB members support each other through the tech interview process. Pods are grouped by target role, meet weekly on your own terms, and you receive personalized coaching insights via Slack each week.

Applications for the **[Season Year]** cohort are open now. Space is limited to 30 members per cohort.
