# The Bridge — Interview Accountability Program

> Connecting where you are to where you want to be.

## Overview

The Bridge is an 8-week virtual accountability program where small pods of 4-5 WBB members support each other through the tech interview process. Pods are grouped by target role, meet weekly on their own terms, and each member receives personalized coaching insights via Slack — powered by automated progress analysis.

**Format**: Virtual, self-paced within a structured 8-week framework
**Pod Size**: 4-5 members, grouped by target role
**Cadence**: 3 cohorts per year — January, May, September (aligned with hiring seasons)
**Cost to Members**: Free
**WBB Staff Effort**: ~4 hours per cohort (kickoff + closing + pod formation)

---

## Program Philosophy

1. **Accountability over instruction** — We don't teach people to code. We create the conditions for people who can code to actually land jobs.
2. **Pods over individuals** — The job search is isolating. Small groups create bonds, motivation, and real practice partners.
3. **Data-driven nudges** — Weekly Notion tracking feeds automated coaching that keeps members focused and balanced.
4. **Flexible structure** — Pods decide how to spend their time together. We provide the menu; they choose the meal.
5. **Clear finish line** — 8 weeks. Enough time to make real progress. Short enough to maintain urgency.

## Who It's For

- WBB community members actively job searching or preparing to interview
- Any experience level (pods are grouped by target role, not seniority)
- Must commit to weekly pod meetings and Notion progress logging
- Must be a member of the WBB Slack workspace

## Pod Formation

Pods are grouped by **target role** so that practice and conversation are relevant:

- **Frontend / Full-Stack Web**
- **Backend / Infrastructure**
- **Mobile Development**
- **Data (Analytics, Engineering, Science)**
- **Design (UX/UI, Product Design)**
- **Product / Program Management**

If a role category has fewer than 4 applicants, members are combined with the closest adjacent group. If a category has more than 5, it splits into two pods.

## 8-Week Structure

### Week 0 — Kickoff (WBB-Hosted, 60 min)

All pods gather for a single virtual kickoff on Google Meet:

- Welcome & program expectations (15 min)
- Intro to the Notion tracker & how logging works (10 min)
- Breakout into pods for icebreakers (20 min)
  - Share: name, target role, where you are in your search, one thing you're nervous about
- Pods schedule their first meeting before leaving (15 min)

**Deliverables**: Each member completes a Week 0 self-assessment in Notion (current skills, target companies, goals for the 8 weeks).

### Weeks 1-2 — Foundation

Focus: Assess where you are and build your plan.

**Suggested pod activities**:
- Resume & LinkedIn review session
- Target company list building (everyone shares 10 companies)
- Identify skill gaps and create a personal study plan
- Practice self-introductions / "tell me about yourself"

**Notion tracking begins** — members log their first weekly entry.

### Weeks 3-6 — The Grind

Focus: Consistent practice, applications, and real interviews.

**Suggested pod activities** (pods choose from this menu each week):

| Format | Description | Time |
|--------|-------------|------|
| Mock Interview Pairs | Take turns interviewing each other (behavioral or technical) | 60-90 min |
| Leetcode Together | Solve 2-3 problems together, discuss approaches | 60 min |
| System Design Jam | One person presents a design, group critiques | 45-60 min |
| Application Sprint | Everyone applies to 3-5 jobs on the call together | 60 min |
| Resume/Portfolio Roast | Constructive feedback on each other's materials | 45 min |
| Interview Debrief | Share experiences from real interviews that week | 30-45 min |
| Salary & Negotiation | Research comp ranges, practice negotiation scripts | 45 min |
| Company Research | Deep-dive on a company: culture, interview process, prep strategy | 45 min |

Members receive **automated weekly coaching DMs** in Slack every Monday based on their Notion data.

### Weeks 7-8 — Final Sprint & Close

Focus: Push hard on applications and interviews. Reflect on growth.

**Suggested pod activities**:
- Final mock interview round
- Application blitz
- Retrospective: what worked, what would you change, what surprised you
- Exchange personal contact info to stay connected beyond the program

**Week 8**: Members complete an exit survey (Google Forms). Optional virtual closing ceremony hosted by WBB.

## Weekly Notion Tracking

Members fill out one row per week in the Bridge Tracker Notion database. This data powers the automated coaching.

| Field | What to Log |
|-------|-------------|
| DSA Problems | Number of coding problems practiced |
| System Design Sessions | Number of system design practice sessions |
| Behavioral Prep | Number of behavioral questions practiced |
| Mock Interviews | Number of mock interviews (given + received) |
| Applications Sent | Number of job applications submitted |
| Interviews Scheduled | Number of real interviews on calendar |
| Offers Received | Running total of offers |
| Pod Meeting Attended | Whether you attended this week's pod meeting |
| Focus This Week | What you primarily worked on |
| Confidence (1-5) | How you're feeling about your search |
| Blockers | What's in your way |
| Wins | Celebrations, no matter how small |
| Reflection | Brief weekly note |

**Deadline**: Sunday night each week. Automated coaching runs Monday morning.

---

## Automated Weekly Coaching

Every Monday morning, an automation script:

1. Queries the Bridge Tracker Notion database for the current week's entries
2. Analyzes each member's trajectory across all weeks:
   - **Consistency**: Are they logging regularly? Attending pod meetings?
   - **Balance**: Are they only doing DSA and never applying? Only applying and never practicing?
   - **Momentum**: Is their activity trending up, flat, or declining?
   - **Confidence**: How's their self-reported confidence trending?
   - **Blockers**: Are the same blockers recurring?
3. Sends a personalized Slack DM to each member with:
   - Acknowledgment of their wins
   - 1-2 specific, actionable suggestions
   - A nudge if they're slipping in an area
   - A relevant free resource link when appropriate
4. Sends a pod-level summary to each pod's Slack channel (anonymized aggregate stats)

### Example Coaching DM

> **Your Bridge Week 4 Check-in**
>
> 12 DSA problems this week — that's your best week yet. And 3 mock interviews on top of that. You're putting in the work.
>
> One pattern I'm noticing: you haven't submitted any applications in 2 weeks. The practice is building your skills, but applications are how you convert that prep into real interviews. Could this week's pod session be an Application Sprint?
>
> You mentioned imposter syndrome as a blocker — that's common and valid. You don't need to feel "ready" to apply. You get ready BY interviewing. Start with companies that aren't your top choices to build confidence.
>
> Keep building.
> — We Build Black

## Graduation Criteria

Members who complete The Bridge earn a digital certificate and WBB recognition:

- [ ] Logged progress in at least **6 of 8 weeks**
- [ ] Attended at least **6 of 8 pod meetings**
- [ ] Completed at least **4 mock interviews**
- [ ] Submitted at least **10 job applications**

Members who land a job during or within 60 days after the program are celebrated in #announcements.

## WBB Operational Checklist (Per Cohort)

### Pre-Launch (2 weeks before kickoff)
- [ ] Open applications via Google Forms
- [ ] Promote on WBB social channels and Slack #announcements
- [ ] Review applications and accept members (20-30 cap)

### Launch Week
- [ ] Create Notion tracker database from `bridge-tracker` template
- [ ] Create cohort config in `programs/cohorts/bridge-{year}-{season}.json`
- [ ] Form pods by target role
- [ ] Create Slack channels for cohort + each pod
- [ ] Send welcome messages with Notion tracker link and pod assignments
- [ ] Host virtual kickoff (60 min)
- [ ] Enable weekly coaching job in `daily-jobs.json`

### During Program (Automated)
- [ ] Weekly coaching DMs run automatically every Monday
- [ ] Pod summaries posted automatically every Monday
- [ ] Monitor for any pods going silent (no logs for 2+ weeks)

### Closing
- [ ] Send exit survey (Google Forms)
- [ ] Generate final cohort report from Notion data
- [ ] Host optional virtual closing ceremony
- [ ] Celebrate graduates in #announcements
- [ ] Disable coaching automation for this cohort
- [ ] Archive Slack channels (or leave open for continued connection)

## Metrics to Track

### Per Cohort
- Number of members who graduate (meet all 4 criteria)
- Number of job offers received during the program
- Number of job offers received within 60 days post-program
- Average confidence score trend (Week 1 vs Week 8)
- Pod meeting attendance rate
- Weekly logging completion rate

### Over Time
- Cohort-over-cohort improvement in graduation rate
- Total jobs landed through The Bridge
- Member retention (do Bridge alumni stay active in WBB?)
- Net Promoter Score from exit surveys
