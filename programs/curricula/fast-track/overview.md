# Fast Track Workforce Training Program

Fast Track is WBB's flagship workforce development program, providing participants with the skills, mentorship, and professional network needed to launch careers in tech.

## Structure
- **Duration**: 6 months
- **Tracks**: Android Development, UX Design, Data Analytics
- **Milestones**: 6 milestones × $1,000 stipend each ($6,000 total)
- **Mentorship**: 1:1 mentor matching with industry professionals
- **Internship Pipeline**: Partner placements (e.g., Shopify)

## Partners
- Google (founding partner)
- Shopify (internship placements)
- CareerCircle (job placement support)
